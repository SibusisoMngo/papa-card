Dear Papa,

I do not say it enough, so today it gets said properly. Thank you. For the early mornings, the long days, and all the quiet ways you carried things so the rest of us never had to feel the weight.

✦

You taught me what showing up looks like. Not with big speeches, but by being there, every single time, without making a thing of it. So much of who I am came from watching you, and I am still learning from you.

The patience. The humour. The way you make a hard thing look simple and a small moment feel like it matters. I notice all of it now in a way I could not when I was younger.

Whatever I build, wherever I go, I carry you with me. You are my first hero and still my favourite one.

Love you always,Your kid
